Noteit — Privacy Policy

Noteit stores all of your data locally on your device. Your notes, images, settings, tags, and reminders are saved using Chrome's built-in storage (chrome.storage.local) and never leave your computer.

What we collect

Nothing. Noteit collects no personal information, no usage analytics, no telemetry, and makes no network requests. There is no account, no sign-up, and no server.

Permissions explained

storage / unlimitedStorage — saves your notes (including pasted images) on your device. alarms / notifications — schedules and displays the reminders you set, locally.

Data sharing & sale

We do not transmit, share, sell, or monetize any data — there is no data to share, because none ever leaves your device.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the extension permanently deletes all locally stored data. You can also export a backup of your notes at any time from Settings.
